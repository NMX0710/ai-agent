#### Home late after work — what's the fastest and healthiest dinner?

Prioritize quick, high-protein, low-oil meals.  
**Recommended Recipe: Microwave Steamed Eggs + Spinach Salad**  
**Why:** Ready in under 10 minutes, nutritious and time-saving.

#### No time to cook — how can I prep ingredients in advance?

Marinate meats and pre-chop vegetables for quick stir-frying.  
**Recommended Recipe: Quick Stir-Fried Chicken Breast with Bell Peppers**  
**Why:** With ingredients prepped, it's done in 3 minutes on the stove.

#### How to make a fast and nutritious breakfast?

Prepare breakfast boxes with pre-combined ingredients, ready to heat or cook.  
**Recommended Recipe: Overnight Oats with Milk**  
**Why:** Prep the night before, just heat or eat directly in the morning.

#### Any recommendations for packed lunch?

Choose foods that store well, are balanced, and won’t spoil easily.  
**Recommended Recipe: Pan-Fried Tofu with Mixed Vegetables Bento**  
**Why:** Rich in plant protein and veggies, flavorful without being greasy.

#### How to avoid poor nutrition from too much takeout?

Cook at home at least 2–3 times per week to replenish fiber and nutrients.  
**Recommended Recipe: Stir-Fried Shrimp with Broccoli + Mixed Grain Rice**  
**Why:** High protein, low fat, easy to prep, and ready in 10 minutes.
