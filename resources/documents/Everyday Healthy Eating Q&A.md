#### How to structure a healthy daily diet?

Include carbohydrates, protein, fruits and vegetables, and moderate fats. Keep oil and salt to a minimum.  
**Recommended Recipe: Mixed Grains with Stir-Fried Trio Veggies**  
**Why:** Whole grains and colorful vegetables offer rich and balanced nutrition.

#### How to reduce health risks from frequent takeout?

Choose dishes with less oil and salt, control portion size, and order extra vegetables.  
**Recommended Recipe: Stir-Fried Tomato & Eggs with Greens**  
**Why:** Simple to make, common ingredients, and highly nutritious.

#### Is skipping breakfast bad for health?

Yes, it can lead to unstable blood sugar and reduced concentration.  
**Recommended Recipe: Whole Wheat Toast with Fried Egg + Milk**  
**Why:** Provides enough energy to start the day with a balanced meal.

#### How to increase vegetable intake in daily meals?

Include at least one type of vegetable in each meal; try adding veggies to dishes or soups.  
**Recommended Recipe: Mushroom Tofu Soup**  
**Why:** Low in calories, high in fiber, and good for digestive health.

#### How to adjust your diet after a heavy meal or social event?

Eat light the next day, drink more water, and eat more vegetables.  
**Recommended Recipe: Winter Melon & Shrimp Soup**  
**Why:** Low-fat and helps flush excess water, restoring body balance.
