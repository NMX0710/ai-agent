#### How to control calories during fat loss without feeling hungry?

Choose high-fiber, low-calorie foods to boost satiety.  
**Recommended Recipe: Chicken Breast & Veggie Salad**  
**Why:** Chicken breast is low-fat and high-protein. Paired with veggies, it’s filling yet low in calories.

#### Will skipping dinner help me lose weight faster?

Skipping dinner is not recommended—it may lead to overeating the next day.  
**Recommended Recipe: Seaweed Tofu Soup**  
**Why:** Light and low-fat, rich in protein and minerals. Perfect for a light dinner.

#### What are good staple foods for fat loss?

Opt for low-GI carbohydrates like brown rice or oats.  
**Recommended Recipe: Brown Rice with Scrambled Eggs & Broccoli**  
**Why:** Balanced nutrition, slow energy release, and stable blood sugar.

#### Can I eat snacks while trying to lose weight?

Yes, but choose low-calorie, low-sugar healthy snacks.  
**Recommended Recipe: Unsweetened Yogurt with Crushed Nuts**  
**Why:** Provides quality fats and protein, curbs appetite.

#### What to do when I crave sweets during fat loss?

Pick healthy alternatives to reduce calorie intake.  
**Recommended Recipe: Banana Oat Pancakes**  
**Why:** Naturally sweet, high in fiber and complex carbs—satisfying yet low-burden.
